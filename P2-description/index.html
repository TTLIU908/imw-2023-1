Inspirations

Since the theme is about environmental protection, this gave me the first idea of the fireflies that were everywhere in the woods in the countryside when I was a kid, but now they have disappeared because of environmental problems. So I would like to make the background of the website have an effect of fireflies in the sky. Once to remember the scenes of the past and promote environmental protection.

To echo the background and the text, I chose two more vibrant fonts and two main colors of fireflies as the main color scheme for the text.
